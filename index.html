Umwcpscadvising

This is the CPSC advising software

Download as .zip Download as .tar.gz View on GitHub

This project is for the University of Mary Washington Computer Science Department students and advisors. It is to aid students and professors in the selection of courses. The professors will be able to better collect data and analyze that data to help upperclassmen or graduating seniors get into classes they need to complete the UMW CPSC track of their choice.
